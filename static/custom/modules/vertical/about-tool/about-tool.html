Unit of measure

Percentage of active population. ‘Active population’ is defined by EUROSTAT as follows: “The labour force or workforce or economically active population, also shortened to active population, includes both employed (employees and self-employed) and unemployed people, but not the economically inactive, such as pre-school children, school children, students and pensioners.”
